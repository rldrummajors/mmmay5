Marching Spotlight!

One way to get better at Marching Band is by watching other Marching Bands that possess the "next-level" skills that we are trying to obtain! Marching Spotlight features various spectacular High School Marching Bands, College Bands, DCI groups, and possibly even WGI performances!

Year: 2025

Show Name: Only The Broken Can See

Group: RCC

Location: WGI Percussion World Champions

Experience Level: WGI Independent World

Context: "Only The Broken Can See" is an exploration of self-discovery and transformation, following a seeker's journey through abstract landscapes. With the holding or sharing of truths symbolized by white boxes, RCC's performance exemplifies the themes of acceptance and sacredness in vulnerability, ultimately earning them a record-breaking score of 99.163.
